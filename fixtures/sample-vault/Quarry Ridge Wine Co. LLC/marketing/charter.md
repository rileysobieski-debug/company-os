# Marketing charter — Quarry Ridge Wine Co. LLC

_Authored by the marketing manager during Phase 6 onboarding. Ratified by the founder 2026-04-09._

## Mandate

Marketing is responsible for building and maintaining the audience that buys Quarry Ridge wine through the direct-to-consumer channel. This means the brand voice, the owned-channel content, the press relationships, and the mailing-list mechanics that turn wine-literate buyers into allocated customers.

## First-month priorities

1. Finalize the brand voice guide. Ship a 6-page document that a copywriter and a designer can execute against without follow-up. Target: end of month 1.
2. Stand up the beta storefront. No wine available for purchase yet; mailing-list signup, founder-voice "coming soon" content, age gate, and compliance pixel test. Target: end of month 1.
3. Identify the first 150 seed names for the mailing list from the founder's existing wine-trade contacts. Confirm email permissions per US CAN-SPAM and send a one-sentence opening message. Target: mid-month 1.
4. Draft the first press-pitch list for post-release outreach. Do not send yet; the wines will be more pitchable once bottled. Target: end of month 1.

## Will NOT do

- Run paid acquisition in year one. Mailing-list growth comes from referral and earned media.
- Build a tasting-room narrative or any content that implies Quarry Ridge is a hospitality business.
- Frame wines as luxury goods, even as a stylistic tactic.
- Create content that overstates what the technical sheet substantiates.

## Cross-department asks

- **Operations**: please confirm the release window for the 2026 vintage by end of month 2. The marketing calendar backs into this date; every week of uncertainty costs real planning time.
- **Finance**: please share the approved annual marketing budget by end of month 1. Guide: my working number is $28,000 for year one, weighted toward technology stack and earned-media PR, with near-zero paid acquisition.

## Open questions escalated to the founder

- Press strategy: aim for earned media in the 2026 lead-up, or stay quiet until bottled? This is a brand-integrity decision, not a marketing tactical call.
- Mailing-list framing for the first 150 names: warm outreach from you personally, or cold-adjacent from me with your endorsement? Both work; the choice is about your bandwidth.
