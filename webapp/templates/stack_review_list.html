Departmental stack reviews

Board-led synthesis across all onboarding artifacts. Proposes new departments, consolidations, terminations, and orchestrator amendments — you approve individually.

How this works

When every active department finishes onboarding, the Board (6 voices) reads the entire corpus — every skill-scope, every founder brief, every charter, plus your priorities and convictions — and produces a review document.

Reviews are propose-only. Each proposal (new dept / consolidation / termination / orchestrator amendment) is a separate item you can accept, reject, or defer individually. Accepting a new-department proposal creates a dormant dept stub AND auto-drafts the arrival note so it's ready when you navigate to the new dept's onboarding page.
